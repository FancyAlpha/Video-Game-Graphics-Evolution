Tennis for Two (1958)

Then came along "Tennis for two" by Physicist William Higinbotham in October 1958, a primitive tennis simulator game played on an oscilloscope-like device, which graphics like only had a green streak behind the ball. It was mean't to be a display at the Brookhaven National Laberatory.
